How it works

lolcounter.us is built using a probabalistic model that analyzes the winrates of various champion matchups to suggest the champion pick that will maximize your chance of winning your game.

There are currently 138 league of legends champions, each with their unique strengths and weaknesses. lolcounter.us takes into account not just the strength of each individual champion pick, but also the viabillity of each champion in conjunction with each of your allied champions. Similarly, each champion you could pick is compared to each enemy pick, with special emphasis placed on your enemy laner.

Counterpicking is a key part of league of legends meta game strategy, and is neccesary in maximizing your chances at victory. lolchampion.us seeks to quantify these matchup strengths, and helps you make champion pick decisions not on vague feelings but on the actual data recorded from hundreds of thousands of games.
